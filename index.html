Features

Everything your ears need

🎚️

Frequency Explorer

Slide through the full human hearing range — 20 Hz to 20,000 Hz — and hear every tone with pure sine-wave accuracy.

📈

Hearing Range Test

Sweep from 2 kHz to 20 kHz and tap the moment silence hits. Find your personal hearing cutoff and see how it compares by age.

🎭

Saboteur Mode

One person listens with eyes closed, another secretly mutes the tone. Catch the silences — or don't. A two-player hearing game built right in.

📊

Test History

Every hearing test is saved locally on your device. Track changes over time and see which ear performs better.

🔇

Ad-Free Option

Enjoy the full experience without interruptions. Remove ads with a one-time purchase — no subscription, no tricks.

🔒

Private by Design

All test results stay on your device. No account required, no data sent to servers.

🎭 Saboteur Mode

Can you catch the silence?

Phone flat on the table. Tester closes their eyes. The Saboteur holds the top panel to secretly mute the tone — any time, any duration. Tap when you hear silence. The sine wave tells the whole story.
